Leon's Fortnite Improvement Guide

A guide to help Leon get better at Fortnite, because after two years, it's time to step it up!

1. Practice Building in Creative Maps

Building is one of the most important skills in Fortnite. Leon, you should spend time in Creative mode practicing your building. Focus on common build techniques like 90s, tunneling, and quick edits.

Beluga the cat says, "If I can be a meme star, you can be a Fortnite star!"

2. Aim Training

Good aim is crucial in Fortnite. Use aim training maps in Creative or third-party aim trainers like Aim Lab or Kovaak's. Focus on improving your accuracy and reaction time to win more fights.

3. Zero Build Logic Training & Decision Making

In Zero Build mode, positioning and decision-making are key. Learn how to rotate effectively, use cover, and make smart decisions in high-pressure situations. Play more games in Zero Build mode to improve your decision-making.
